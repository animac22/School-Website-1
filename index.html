School Life

School Playground

Was built in 2012

The school playground has lots of things to do. For example, we have a gaga ball pit in the conter of the place. We also have hopscotch lines on the pavement. There is also a red light, green light space close to the gaga ball pit. If you need to catch your breath or you want to relax, there is a structure with a roof to provide shade. Inside the structure, there is one bench. there is also a ton of space to sit on the ground. If you want to play soccer, there is a big field with two goals on each end. there is also a large playing field for all your favorite games. Lastly, there is the playground, of course, which has a lot of things you can run and jump on including: monkey bars, tic tac toe, and slides.

School Library

Was built in 2012

The school library has books for everyone. Wether it is 6th graders who want a mystery series or if its a 2nd grader that is looking for a fantasy book to enjoy. Also, you can reasearch in the library about different topics for school. Finally, you can try out new genres instead of the old ones you are bored of reading. You can really enjoy a good book there.

School Cafeteria

Was built in 2012

The school cafeteria allows you to eat your lunch and talk with friends. The seats are clean and the tables are wiped every time a new class comes to eat. There is also a school lunch menu. It has something for everyone to eat. you can also buy snacks such as: chips, ice cream, and other additional things such as water bottles. The school food is really good. Plus, you can ask your parents to come eat lunch with you.

School Achievements

Lindsey Elementary Bowling Team
Won first place every year in school district bowling competition

This competition includes all of the schools in Allen ISD, also known as the Allen Independent School District. Our school has won the first place award every year. This is all done by the amazing PE coach and the impresive bowling team. Every year, the 5th and 6th graders have an opportunity to sign up for the bowling team. If their name gets drawn, they get to be on the bowling team. There will be two practices before the competition day.
